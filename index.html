Readings

Week 1

  • Weiser, The Computer for the 21st Century:

    Weiser, M. (1991, September). The Computer for the 21st Century. Scientific American, 265(3), 94–100.

  • The Coming Age of Calm Technology:

    Weiser, M., & Brown, J. S. (1997). The Coming Age of Calm Technology. In P. J. Denning & R. M. Metcalfe, Beyond Calculation (pp. 75–85). https://doi.org/10.1007/978-1-4612-0685-9_6.

  • Yvonne Rogers commentary on Calm Technology, ten years later

    Rogers, Y. (2006). Moving on from Weiser’s Vision of Calm Computing: Engaging UbiComp Experiences. In P. Dourish & A. Friday (Eds.), UbiComp 2006: Ubiquitous Computing (Vol. 4206, pp. 404–421). https://doi.org/10.1007/11853565_24

  • Optional Reading:

    Jones-Rooy, A. (2019, July 24). I’m a data scientist who is skeptical about data. Retrieved August 11, 2019, from Quartz website: https://qz.com/1664575/is-data-science-legit/

    Get a little skeptical of the big data.
